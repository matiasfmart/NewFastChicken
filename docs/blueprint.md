# **App Name**: Fast Chicken POS

## Core Features:

- Order Entry: Allows the cashier to create and customize orders including combos, individual items, and delivery options.
- Real-time Inventory: Automatically updates stock levels as orders are placed, with low stock warnings for cashiers and administrators.
- Automated Discount Application: System automatically applies pre-configured discounts to combos and individual items.
- Ticket Generation: Generates customer and kitchen tickets with order details, prices, and delivery type.
- End-of-Day Reports: Automatically generates detailed sales and order reports at the end of each business day.
- Menu Management: Administrator interface for managing menu items, combo configurations, pricing, and discounts.
- Stock Forecasting Tool: Uses historical sales data to predict demand for ingredients, assisting the administrator with inventory planning by generating restock suggestions. An AI tool determines what factors it should use for forecasting such as time of day, and popularity of an ingredient in different dishes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated yellow (#FFC107) to evoke feelings of hunger, energy and happiness.
- Background color: Light yellow (#FFF8E1), for a bright, clean, inviting atmosphere that ensures legibility.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF9800) to highlight key actions such as order confirmation and important notifications.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a clear and legible sans-serif font for easy readability in both the user interface and printed tickets.
- Simple and clear icons representing food items, delivery options, and system functions.
- Clean and intuitive layout with a focus on ease of use for both cashiers and administrators. High contrast between text and background colors will ensure readability. Modals for customizations.
- Subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback on user interactions and system processes. For example, animated checkmark to confirm an action, etc.
